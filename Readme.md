### 1. üë• Talent Acquisition Agent (HR Domain)
**Problem**: Traditional recruitment is inefficient, biased, and time-consuming
- **Impact**: 60% time spend on screening, 42-day average time-to-hire, $240B lost annually due to poor hiring decisions
- **Solution**: Multi-agent system handling complete recruitment workflow
- **Tech Stack**: AutoGen/LangGraph, GPT-4, PostgreSQL, FastAPI, Sentence Transformers, Whisper

An autonomous AI agent system that revolutionizes talent acquisition by handling the complete recruitment workflow. The system consists of multiple specialized agents working in coordination:

**Key Features:**
- Resume screening with bias detection
- Automated interview scheduling and conducting
- Technical assessment and decision support
- ATS integration and candidate engagement

**Core Functionalities**:
- **Resume Screening Agent**: Analyzes resumes against job requirements with skill matching and experience evaluation
- **Interview Scheduling Agent**: Coordinates calendars, sends invitations, and manages rescheduling
- **Pre-screening Agent**: Conducts initial phone/video interviews using conversational AI
- **Assessment Agent**: Administers technical tests and evaluates responses
- **Decision Support Agent**: Provides hiring recommendations with bias detection
- **Communication Agent**: Maintains candidate engagement throughout the process

**Key Features:**

## NLP Projects

### 5. üìä E-commerce Reviews Sentiment Analysis (E-commerce Domain)
**Problem**: Businesses can't effectively analyze feedback at scale from the massive volume of customer reviews
- **Impact**: 1.5M daily Amazon reviews, Fake reviews constitute 30-40% of all online reviews
- **Solution**: Advanced sentiment analysis with aspect-based insights
- **Tech Stack**: RoBERTa, spaCy, Beautiful Soup (bs4), Elasticsearch

An advanced NLP system that provides comprehensive sentiment analysis and business intelligence from e-commerce reviews. The system goes beyond basic positive/negative classification to provide nuanced insights about specific product aspects, customer emotions, and business recommendations.

**Core Functionalities**:
- **Aspect-Based Sentiment Analysis**: Analyzes sentiment for specific product features (battery life, design, price, etc.)
- **Emotion Detection**: Identifies specific emotions (frustration, delight, disappointment, excitement)
- **Fake Review Detection**: Uses machine learning to identify suspicious or fraudulent reviews
- **Trend Analysis**: Tracks sentiment changes over time and identifies emerging issues
- **Competitive Intelligence**: Compares sentiment across competitor products
- **Actionable Insights**: Generates specific recommendations for product improvements

**Key Features:**
- Aspect-based sentiment analysis
- Fake review detection
- Real-time sentiment monitoring
- Competitive intelligence

### 6. üìÑ Contract Analysis NER System (Financial Domain)
**Problem**: Manual contract review is time-consuming and error-prone
- **Impact**: 2-6 hours per contract, professionals spend 41% of their time on document review
- **Solution**: NER-based contract analysis with risk assessment
- **Tech Stack**: Legal-BERT, Huggingface, Elasticsearch, OCR - pytesseract

An advanced NLP system specifically designed for comprehensive contract analysis using Named Entity Recognition and relationship extraction. The system automatically identifies and extracts critical contract elements, analyzes risks, and provides actionable insights for legal and business teams.

**Core Functionalities**:
- **Entity Extraction**: Identifies parties, dates, monetary amounts, obligations, and legal terms
- **Clause Classification**: Categorizes contract clauses by type and importance
- **Risk Assessment**: Analyzes contract language for potential risks and unfavorable terms
- **Obligation Tracking**: Extracts and monitors contractual obligations and deadlines
- **Compliance Checking**: Validates contracts against regulatory requirements and company policies
- **Contract Comparison**: Identifies differences between contract versions and standard templates

**Key Features:**
- Advanced entity extraction
- Risk assessment engine
- Obligation tracking
- Contract comparison and redlining
